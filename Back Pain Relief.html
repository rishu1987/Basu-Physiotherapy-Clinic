Back Pain Relief: Effective Strategies for a Healthier Spine

Dr.Anshu Srivastava October 15, 2023

Understanding Back Pain

Back pain is one of the most common health complaints worldwide, affecting people of all ages and lifestyles. Whether it stems from poor posture, an injury, or chronic conditions like arthritis, back pain can disrupt daily life and limit mobility.

Common Causes

  • Muscle or ligament strain from heavy lifting
  • Poor posture during work or leisure
  • Herniated or degenerated discs
  • Chronic conditions like arthritis
  • Sedentary lifestyle effects

Immediate Relief Strategies

Cold Therapy

Apply ice packs for 15-20 minutes to reduce inflammation during acute phases.

Gentle Stretching

Try cat-cow stretches or knee-to-chest exercises to relieve tension.

Long-Term Prevention

Core Strengthening
Planks, bridges, and Pilates
Ergonomic Setup
Proper workstation alignment
Sleep Position
Side sleeping with knee pillow

When to Seek Help

  • Pain lasting >2 weeks
  • Leg numbness/weakness
  • Bladder/bowel issues
  • Unexplained weight loss
